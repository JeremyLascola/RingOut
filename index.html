Ring Game! ALPHA (Better name TBD)
Bring the white ball through all the gaps in the rings!
The closer to the center the ball is the more score you accumulate
You can not move the ball in or out, the rings will push the ball out when you are bad at this game.
Controls:
Move the mouse and the ball follows in the path of a circle
